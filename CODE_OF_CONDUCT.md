# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

We are committed to providing a welcoming and productive environment for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We expect all participants to treat each other with respect and professionalism.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the project and its community

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **conduct@thingwire.dev**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.
